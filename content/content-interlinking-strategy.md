# N3uralia Chile SEO - Content Interlinking Strategy

## Strategic Framework

### Linking Pyramid Architecture

```
                    Blog Posts (24 pages)
                    ↙  ↓  ↓  ↓  ↓  ↓  ↘
        Industry Verticals (5 pages)
                    ↙  ↓  ↓  ↓  ↘
            Money Pages (3 pages)
                    ↙  ↓  ↘
                Core Site
```

**Flow of Authority**: Blog → Verticals → Money Pages → Core
**Anchor Text Distribution**: 60% branded, 30% exact-match, 10% long-tail
**Link Frequency**: 2-4 internal links per 500 words

## Part 5: Link Placement Strategy

### Recommended Link Placements (by page section)

#### Money Pages (High Authority)

1. **Introduction/Hero** (1 link)
   - Best for: Related money page or primary CTA
   - Anchor: Branded or exact-match
   - Example: "...usando <a>soluciones agenticas</a>..."

2. **Body/Main Content** (1-2 links)
   - Best for: Related content, blog posts
   - Anchor: Long-tail or contextual
   - Example: "Learn more in our <a href>guide to implementation</a>"

3. **Related Solutions** (1 link)
   - Best for: Complementary money page
   - Anchor: Exact-match
   - Example: "También ofrecemos <a>automatización IA</a>"

4. **CTA Section** (1 link)
   - Best for: Methodology/trust page
   - Anchor: Branded
   - Example: "<a>Ver cómo trabajamos</a>"

#### Blog Posts (Medium Authority)

1. **Introduction** (1 link)
   - Best for: Primary money page or vertical
   - Anchor: Contextual/long-tail

2. **Body** (1-2 links)
   - Best for: Related blog posts, verticals
   - Anchor: Mixed

3. **Conclusion** (1 link)
   - Best for: Money page CTA
   - Anchor: Branded or exact-match

#### Vertical Pages (Medium Authority)

1. **Introduction** (1 link)
   - Best for: Primary money page
   - Anchor: Exact-match

2. **Body** (2-3 links)
   - Best for: Blog posts, related content
   - Anchor: Mixed

3. **CTA** (1 link)
   - Best for: Money page
   - Anchor: Branded
