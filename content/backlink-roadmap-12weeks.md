## Ongoing Maintenance (After Week 12)

### Monthly Activities:
- [ ] Monitor backlink health (no broken links)
- [ ] Respond to new link opportunities
- [ ] Create 1-2 new case studies
- [ ] Maintain association memberships
- [ ] Track rankings monthly

### Content Repurposing:
- Case studies → Blog posts → Guest posts → Press releases
- Create "Link bait" content (lists, original research, tools)

### Long-term Strategy:
- Target 50+ backlinks by Month 6
- Maintain 100+ backlinks by Month 12
- Focus on topical relevance (not just quantity)

---

## Key Success Factors

1. **Timing**: Start Phase 1 immediately for Q3 visibility
2. **Relationships**: Build genuine partnerships (not transactional)
3. **Quality > Quantity**: 40 links from DA 50+ better than 100 from DA 10
4. **Relevance**: Chilean + AI/Tech links > generic links
5. **Consistency**: Sustained effort over 12 weeks > one-time campaigns

---

## Contingency Plan

| Risk | Mitigation |
|------|-----------|
| No media interest | Leverage PR agency, focus on events instead |
| Budget constraints | Skip event sponsorship, focus on organic links |
| Slow backlink velocity | Increase guest post outreach, expand directories |
| Broken link risk | Monitor monthly, request link updates |
