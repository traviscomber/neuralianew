## Content Hooks (for outreach)

### 1. Press Release - "N3uralia raises $X for AI agents in Chile"
- Who: N3uralia
- What: Funding/partnership announcement
- Why: Expanding IA services to Chilean businesses
- Target: All Tier 1 & 2 sites

### 2. Expert Commentary - "AI Automation Trends in 2026"
- Position: CEO/founder as AI expert
- Topic: What Chilean businesses should know about AI
- Distribution: Tech publications, business journals

### 3. Case Study Press - "Chilean Mining Company Reduces Costs 70% with AI"
- Story: Real case study (anonymized)
- Angle: Innovation in traditional industries
- Distribution: Industry publications + business media

### 4. Research Report - "State of AI in Chilean SMEs 2026"
- Conduct survey of Chilean business leaders
- Publish findings + distribute to media
- Generate 15-20 backlinks from coverage

### 5. Thought Leadership - CEO Blog/Interview Series
- Topics: AI ethics, responsible AI, local implementation
- Pitch to: Business media, tech podcasts, newsletters
