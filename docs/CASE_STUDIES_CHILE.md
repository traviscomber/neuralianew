# Case Studies - N3uralia Chile

## Case Study 1: Retail Santiago - 70% Cost Reduction

**Company**: Cadena retail multi-sucursal Santiago  
**Industry**: Retail  
**Challenge**: 50 customer service reps, $200M CLP/year in salaries + overhead  
**Solution**: AI agents for 24/7 customer support, order processing, returns management  
**Results**:
- Cost reduction: 70% ($140M CLP savings/year)
- Implementation time: 8 weeks
- Customer satisfaction: +25% NPS improvement
- ROI: Positive from month 6

**Metrics**:
- Tickets per agent: 500 → 5,000 per month (AI agents)
- Average response time: 5 minutes → instant
- Resolution rate: 85% → 92%
