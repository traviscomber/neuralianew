# Social Media Strategy 2026 - N3uralia Chile

## LinkedIn Strategy (Primary Channel)

### Content Mix
- 40% Educational (tips, guides, case studies)
- 30% Thought leadership (CEO insights, trends)
- 20% Company updates (case wins, launches)
- 10% Engagement (comments, conversations)

### Weekly Posts

**Monday**: Industry insight or trend (AI in mining, retail, logistics)
**Wednesday**: Case study or success story
**Friday**: Tip or how-to content

Example posts:

**Monday Example**:
"We just helped a mining company in Antofagasta reduce downtime by 50% using predictive maintenance AI.

Key lesson: Traditional maintenance is reactive. Intelligent agents are predictive.

Result: $300M CLP saved in year 1.

What's your biggest operational bottleneck?"

**Wednesday Example**:
"Case study: How a Santiago retail company went from 50 customer service reps to 8 strategic staff using AI agents.

70% cost reduction. 25% NPS improvement. Real results.

Full case study: [link]"

**Friday Example**:
"Quick tip: When evaluating AI agents for your business, ask:
1. Can they integrate with existing systems? (Critical)
2. Do they learn over time? (Quality matters)
3. Is there human escalation? (Safety first)
4. What's the actual ROI timeline? (Realistic expectations)

Most vendors won't answer #4 honestly. We do."

### Hashtag Strategy

Primary: #AgentesIA #AutomatizacionIA #IAChile
Secondary: #Minería #Retail #Logística #Empresas

### Engagement Goals

- Post reach: 1,000-5,000 per post
- Engagement rate: 5-10%
- Follower growth: 50-100 per month
- Profile visits: 200-500 per week

## Twitter/X Strategy (Secondary)

### Content: Quick takes, links to blog, industry news reactions

**Daily**:
- 1-2 AI news reactions
- 1 tip or insight
- Retweet relevant industry content

Example tweets:

"New research shows AI automation drives 40-70% cost reduction in operations. In Chile, we're seeing 300% ROI in year 1. The question isn't if, but when you adopt. [blog-link]"

"Myth: AI will replace your employees.
Reality: AI replaces repetitive tasks. Smart companies use AI to free teams for strategy.
We've seen 100% team satisfaction post-AI implementation."

### Engagement Goals

- Tweet impressions: 500-2,000 per tweet
- Engagement rate: 1-3%
- Growth: 50+ followers per month

---

## Email Strategy (Newsletter)

### Frequency: Weekly (Sunday 10am Chile time)

### Format
- 1 featured blog post
- 2-3 industry news + links
- 1 quick tip
- 1 CTA (case study download, consultation request)

### Segmentation

- Segment 1: Industry-specific (mining, retail, logistics)
- Segment 2: Geography-specific (Santiago, regions)
- Segment 3: Engagement level (highly engaged vs re-engagement)

### Goals

- Open rate: 25-35%
- Click rate: 5-8%
- Conversion rate: 1-2% (to consultation booking)
- Subscriber growth: 100+ per month

---

## Content Repurposing

Blog post → LinkedIn post + Twitter threads + Email snippet + Newsletter feature

Example: "ROI de Agentes IA" blog post becomes:
- 3 LinkedIn posts (different angles)
- 5 tweets (part of thread)
- Newsletter feature (week 1)
- Email segment (mining companies) (week 2)
- Case study distribution (week 3)
